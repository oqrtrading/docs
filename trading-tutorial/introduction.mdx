This tutorial walks you through the ideas you need to trade on Valence. We’ll build from the order book and your position to **fair** and **edge**, then to **consolidated** views and **consolidated position**—and why that one number matters.

By the end you’ll have a single mental model for the market, your exposure, and your orders. Each chapter introduces one concept and builds on the last, so you can read it straight through like a book.

**What we’ll cover:**

1. **The order book** — What you see when you open a market: bids, asks, and levels.
2. **Position** — Your net exposure: long, short, and how we express it in one number.
3. **Fair and edge** — Your valuation (fair) and your advantage (edge).
4. **One market, two legs** — Why we combine yes and no into one view.
5. **The consolidated orderbook** — One book from multiple legs, in one unit.
6. **Your consolidated position** — One number for your net exposure per group (and why it matters for collateral and getting your money back).
7. **Why it matters** — Risk, P&amp;L, sizing, and one model end to end.
8. **Putting it together** — How to use this when you trade.

Ready? Start with [1. The order book](/trading-tutorial/order-book).
