---
title: "4. One market, two legs"
description: "Why we combine yes and no into one view."
---

Many prediction markets have **two legs** for the same event: a **yes** contract and a **no** contract. Each leg has its own order book on the venue.

If you only look at the yes book, you miss liquidity on the no side. And your exposure might be split: e.g. long 30 yes and long 70 no. Two books and two positions are hard to reason about. So we combine them into **one** view.
