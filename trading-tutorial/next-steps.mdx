You’ve learned:

- The **order book** is bids and asks at each level; best bid and best ask are “top of book.”
- **Position** is net exposure (long/short); in binaries, yes and no are two sides of the same event.
- **Fair** is your valuation; **edge** is your advantage. You need both to trade well.
- A **consolidated orderbook** merges legs with orientation into one book in one unit (e.g. yes-cents).
- **Consolidated position** is your net exposure per group in that same unit—the number for risk, sizing, and (on venues like Kalshi) collateral and getting your money back when you’re flat.
- The book, consolidated orders (SOR), and consolidated position all align so you have one model end to end.
