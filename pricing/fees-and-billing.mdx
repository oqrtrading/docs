## Pricing

**Valence is free for everyone** until we think adding a pricing model makes sense. There are no platform fees, no minimums, and no billing at this time.

When we introduce pricing, Valence will be charged according to our fee and tax pricing structure. We will notify existing users in advance and update this page with the full pricing model, billing cycles, and payment methods.
