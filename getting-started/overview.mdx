## What Valence is

Valence is for people who need broad trading functions—we try to do everything: execution, market-making, risk, data, and integration in one place.

## Who this is for

- **Traders** who execute and monitor positions
- **Operators** who manage controls and access
- **Developers** who integrate execution workflows
